A bubble tea shop design which had very short delivery timing. The business owner was in a hurry to open the retail with a tight budget, the design was limited to the right choice of floor tiles, wall paint and it also ended up with an exposed ceiling with pre-ordered furniture pieces. We were able to understand the client’s budget constraints, to make adjustments after his feedbacks and to exceed his expectation all within 2 weeks.
因为需要考虑到客人焦急开张的情绪和不太充足的预算，所以最后只在地砖的选择和墙漆的颜色上稍微发挥了一下(当然也是找了经济实用的类型)，在不封天花的情况下快速的递交了设计方案。整体设计加施工准时在两周之内完成，这还是蛮值得庆幸的。
