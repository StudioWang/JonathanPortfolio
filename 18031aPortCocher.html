Wavy Fins 波浪屏风

The great first impression of Niagara Falls’ Trip
惊艳的第一感受

To emphasize the natural beauty of the Niagara Falls, we choose to use curved, marine color metal fins to build the asymmetric art piece in an abstract way of the water waves. The installation is meant to set up an ambiance right at the arrival of the customers which serves as a wonderful starting point for their journey and stay at Niagara Falls. The wonderful sightseeing experience starts with a great first impression.
为了突出尼亚加拉大瀑布宏伟壮丽的景观，我们用一片片海蓝色弯曲的钢板制作了一组艺术屏风去模仿海浪的波纹。这个艺术品的目的是为了让旅客在进入喜来登酒店之前就能感受到尼亚加拉大瀑布的热情与氛围，为他们接下来激动人心的参观埋下伏笔。

Moody Feeling with ground light 在地灯的烘托下，波浪型的艺术屏风立刻增添了一份朦胧感与诗意。
