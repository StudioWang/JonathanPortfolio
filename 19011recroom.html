Rec room


A hybrid entertainment centre that combines arcade with food services.


Airy, spacious mini-chain for pub grub & craft beer plus arcade games, pool tables & live acts, the ideal place to hangout with friends and family on a regular Friday night. The Rec Room’s goal is to be a playful industry vibe playground by merging beer culture, restoration and arcade. In order to highlight the theme, a significant amount of metal structure with raw materials are used in different areas: 1) The black railing embedding on top of the concrete ramp; 2) Edison bulb light canopy floats in front of a giant corrugated red metal box; 3) The millworks of bar & restaurant beneath the metal girders of the exposed ceiling, etc. With lighting fixtures and Signage in the steam punk style, every inch of this space is telling you that the Rec Room is more then just a trendy pub grab, it also has great games, great foods, great social vibes. It is a destination that provides a all-inclusive night out experience.
