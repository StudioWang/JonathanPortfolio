Delta Fredericton Lobby

Renovation of Delta Hotel’s Lobby

We are renovating the existing Delta Hotel Lobby with integration of a new Barbeque restaurant and one central Bar. Design Guide Line and Site Analysis:

Strengths

Waterfront Location; Large two Story Space; Local Festivals draw tourism; Spirits Festival, Craft Beer Festival, Harvest Jazz & Blues Festival, etc.

Weaknesses

Poor Lighting Porgram; Obstructed Waterfront Views; No Food or Drink Presence; No Activities/Action Beyond Lobby Seating; Dead Zones;printer Table, Buisiness Table on Lower Level; People Walk-thru the Lobby in their bathing suits in the summer; Long Queue at Check-in during busy times

Opportunities

Redefining social zones with a purpose; Deliver a Distinct sense of identity' Explore two story space to maximize the energe and activity in the space; Create event space to draw new clientele; Estabilish Food& Drink presence.

Threats

Staffing; Budget, New Thrieving downtown Restaurants & Bars
