Starlight Casino London 星光赌场


A new build Startlight Gateway Casino in London Ontario, CA


One of the most excited project of 2018 that I ever be able to working on as one of the Mackawong’s crew. Gateway is the Entertainment organization who runs all the Casinos in Ontario，CA. Different then all the others brands, Starlight is their favourite. With Starlight, Gateway Casinos has turned up the volume on this brand. Starlight is a celebration of light, colour and sound. We looked towards the sky for inspiration and have used elements of starbursts and fireworks to generate our design vocabulary. A touch of retro influences the design while staying refined and contemporary. These properties have elevated hospitality and amenities for its guest with higher energy and excitement. Ours Design direction has been resumed in 6 different terms as shown:


1.Exploration:
Starlight offers variety of gaming options, entertainment, dining options, and a superior customer experience.

2.Mid-Century Modern:
Starlight is the glamour and sophistication of modern design.

3.Unique:
Starlight’s unique character is a beacon for excitement and energy.

4.Contemprorary Vegas:
Starlight is a contemporary take on the glamour and glitz of "Vegas Style".

5.High Energy:
Starlight has an atmosphere of excitement..

6.Vibrant:
Starlight turns up the volume on light, colour, and sound.


In conclusion, Starlight Casino London is an exciting, vibrant, upscale, full- service urban entertainment resort with old-Vegas ambiance but with the scale and luxury associated with modern Vegas and exceptional customer service.
