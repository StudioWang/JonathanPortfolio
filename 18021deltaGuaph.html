Delta Guelph Hotel's Patio Development

The Land Commectedness Market Fresh Stagecraft

We believe it is important to recognize that Guelph is a global leader in many rights. The city is growing and thriving – demanding interesting experiences and this demand influences the Food and Beverage market. To a large extent the restaurant marketplace is dominated by corporate brands and quick service fast food chains that deliver a tested concept that is pushed into the city. This reality doesn’t align with the fact that Guelph has some of the most direct access to quality ingredients in Southern Ontario. We see that chain restaurants, and other branded concepts have succeeded in positioning themselves as more “Independent” and locally relevant.
Our research indicates that that the Guelph marketplace is responding well to the consistency delivered by corporate brands, but still craves the vibrancy, creativity and storytelling that an independent establishment can offer. A further analysis is as seen in Appendix A. This is not only true of Guelph, but can be seen in many self-confident marketplaces within Southern Ontario. In this way, the Delta Marriott is perfectly poised to meet this demand.
We believe the Delta Marriott restaurant is well positioned to take advantage of the well established volume of Business clientele in the immediate area, the excellent volume of Conference and Event related Hotel Guests as well as becoming a go to place for professionals within the adjacent Research Park and University.
