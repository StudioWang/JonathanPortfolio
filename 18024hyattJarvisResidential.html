Hyatt Jarvis Residential Suites

A nature inspired and elegant 32 storeys high condominium in the heart of downtown Toronto.

Drawing inspiration from urban garden concept, Hyatt Jarvis Residential redefines the idea of living in a highrise building within a densily populated city. Your home is meant for relaxation and personal chargin station after a long day at work or study. It is a secret evasion gateway beyond the simple basic housing need. In order to integrate natural elements and to create an immersive green space experience, the design incorporates many craft furniture pieces and meticulously chosen exotic carpet pattern and graphics.

Design Areas:
⦁ Residential event room
⦁ Residential kids area + theatre room
⦁ Residential e-work area
⦁ Suite concept
⦁ Fitness studio

The overarching theme of nature flows through each and every area of the condominium which tied together, creates a hollistic and complete healthy and sophisticated living experience.
